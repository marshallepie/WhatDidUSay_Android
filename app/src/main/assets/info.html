WhatDidUSay works just like any other audio recorder, except for one big difference: it captures and saves what's already been said.

Using the app

Setting your recording length:

Tap on the Settings icon and use the slider to set your desired recording length. This will apply to all of your recordings until you change it.

Capturing snippets:

Tap on the Start button to start the app running. This switches on the Listening Mode.

Tap on the Capture button to capture what was just said. A snippet (audio recording) of your chosen length (i.e. the last 20 seconds before you hit the button) will be saved at the top of your screen.
You can repeat this process to capture more snippets of conversation as many times as you like while the app is in Listening Mode.

Tap on the Stop button to stop the app running and turn off Listening Mode. No further recordings will be made.

Your saved snippets:

Snippets are saved automatically in numerical order. You can use the buttons to play, pause or delete your snippets.

Tap to rename.
Press and hold to export.
